Can I buy LIC policies online?
Yes! Many LIC policies can now be purchased online through the official LIC website or with the help of a registered LIC advisor like us.
What documents do I need to buy an LIC policy?
You typically need proof of identity (like Aadhaar or PAN), address proof, age proof, recent passport-sized photos, and sometimes income proof depending on the plan.
How do I claim maturity or death benefits?
For maturity claims, submit the original policy bond, ID proof, and maturity form to your servicing branch. For death claims, the nominee should provide the death certificate, claim form, and other required documents. Or you can always reach out to the advisor for help :)
How safe is LIC?
LIC is a government-owned corporation backed by the sovereign guarantee of India, making it one of the safest and most reliable insurers in the India.
Can I switch between LIC plans?
While you cannot directly switch between plans, you can surrender your existing policy (subject to terms) and purchase a new one that better fits your needs. Always consult your LIC advisor before making changes.
